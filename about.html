About Cafe

Nestled just moments away from the bustling heart of Connaught Place, Cine Tree Cafe is more than just a coffee shop — it’s an experience. Inspired by the charm of classic cinema and the comfort of handcrafted coffee, Cine Tree offers a cozy yet vibrant retreat for coffee lovers, foodies, artists, and dreamers alike. From our carefully curated menu of gourmet brews, artisan teas, and mouth-watering bites to our warm, cinematic-inspired interiors, every detail is designed to awaken your senses. Whether you're here for a quick espresso, a leisurely brunch, or an intimate conversation under the soft glow of vintage lights, Cine Tree Cafe welcomes you with open arms and the aroma of freshly brewed perfection.
